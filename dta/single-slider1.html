Uduny Bids Goodbye to Baobab Beach Resort and it's Affiliated Properties

Working at Baobab Beach Resort & Spa has been a wonderful experience that I will never forget. I am very sad to leave the great staff and environment and wish everyone the best success.Friday marked my final day working for ARP Travel Group. ARP Travel Group is a collection of individually operated companies with a common theme of providing exceptional services to visitors across East Africa.While I’m excited about the new opportunity, a big part of me is sad to be saying goodbye to amazing co-workers. I can’t tell you how much I’ve enjoyed our time working together, and how much I value the friendship you’ve shown me over the past 4 years.

I want to pass my gratitude to Mr Mohammed Hersi who believed in me & gave me the opportunity to serve the company. I’ve learned so much from his experience, advice, and guidance over the past years and my time spent as a Marketing Executive. I know I’ll carry the things I’ve learned here with me to my next adventure, and I’ll always look back on this experience with a lot of fondness. High-grade mentor!

To the entire ARP family, thank you for making me part of your family. You took a chance on me when I was fresh. Thank you for seeing my potential and always giving me new challenges and learning opportunities. You have given me the confidence I need to take the next step in my career.I will always be so proud of what we have built & accomplished together at the company.

The company consisted of:

  • Baobab Beach Resort & Spa
  • Salt Lick Safari Lodge
  • Taita Hills Safari Resort & Spa
  • Pollman’s Tours & Travel
